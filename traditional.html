Traditional Checklists

Yoruba Wedding List



  • Bride price

  • Box of Cloths

  • Fruits in well decorated Baskets

  • Bottles of Wine (in even numbers)

  • Carton of sweets

  • Gallon of Palm oil

  • Gallon of Groundnut Oil

  • Tubers of Yam (Number as requested)

  • Cola nut

  • Dried fish

  • Adun

  • Carton of biscuits

  • A bible (If Christian), Quran (If Moslem)

  • Engagement ring

  • 2 cartoons of Sugar

  • Bag of Salt

  • Proposal Letter

  • Crates of mineral drinks and other assorted drinks

  • Umbrella

Igbo Wedding List



  • Bride price

  • Box of Cloths

  • Request by elders of the village

  • Request by women of the village

  • Request by youths of the village

  • Funds for catering for a reception

Bini Wedding List



  • Bag of rice

  • Bag of garri

  • 20 liters of palm oil

  • 60 tubers of yam

  • Bunches of plantain

  • Bush Meat

  • 20 liters of palm wine

  • 20 liters of groundnut oil

  • One pack of sugar

  • Bottle of honey

  • One bag of Salt

  • Schnapps

  • Assorted drinks

  • 40 pieces of kola nut

  • Bride price (N24)

  • Money for the bride’s mother (N20)

  • Money for the youths of the extended family

Ibibio/Annang Wedding List



  • White Horse(3 in 1)

  • Saint Remiss

  • Schnapps(Number of Cartoons required)

  • Palm Wine

  • Wine drinks

  • Assorted Drinks

  • Bag Basin full of crayfish

  • Small Basin full of pepper

  • A bag of Salt

  • Kolanut

  • Goat

  • Second Goat(if bride is pregnant)

  • Bride price(N50)

  • Consultation fee(the amount requested for depends on the bride’s family)

  • Trunk box full of clothing, under wears, accessories, make up kit etc for bride’s mother to dress with on the wedding day
