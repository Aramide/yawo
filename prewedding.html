Pre-Wedding List

My Wedding Date



  • Present both families with options

  • Both Families agree on prefered dates

  • Church/Mosque gives options

  • Pick the convenient option

My Wedding Budget



  • What type of wedding am I having?

  • How much are we (My Groom and I) willing to spend?

  • Who is picking what bill (For example Aunty Yemi may be picking up the bill for the wedding dress)

  • What bill would I and My Groom have to pick
